Instead of d00 roll 2D on the random ability score table once for each of the eight abilities.

| 2D    | Score | Comments |
|:------|:---:|:---|
| 2-4   |  35%  | Feeble |
| 5-7   |  40%  | Low |
| 8-9   |  45%  | Below Average |
| 10-11 |  50%  | Typical |
| 12-13 |  55%  | Above Average |
| 14-15 |  60%  | High |
| 16-17 |  65%  | Good |
| 18-19 |  70%  | Excellent |
| 20    |  75%  | Remarkable |
